The Best Movies according to Jha-Two

My Top Three movies of all time:

Donny Darko

Donnie Darko, an awkward teenager, befriends Frank, a figure in a bunny costume only he can see, who informs Donnie that the world will end in 28 days, 6 hours, 42 minutes, and 12 seconds.

A Sci-Fi Thriller movie starring the young Jake Gyllenhall. It is based on a creepy pasta of a Bunny, and includes the element of Time travel to give the viewer a good movie session.

Tenet

When a few objects that can be manipulated and used as weapons in the future fall into the wrong hands, a CIA operative, known as the Protagonist, must save the world.

Yet another Christopher Nolan blockbuster, Tenet is a Sci-Fi Action Thriller Film. The concept of time manipulation is used and the ending and the start of the movie are shown parallely, only for it all to be concluded in the middle.

No Time To Die

After leaving the active services, James Bond leads a peaceful life in Jamaica. However, a call for help from an old friend triggers a confrontation with a treacherous enemy.

How can I not add a James Bond Film to the list. This man, written by Ian Fleming, has been my idol and Daniel Craig flawlessly potrayed the character of Bond. This film is the conclusion of Craig Bond saga and is the only Bond Film, which has shown the prospect of having a female, black James Bond in the future, making the role culturaly and diverse and thus connecting more and more people with the Bond Identity.
